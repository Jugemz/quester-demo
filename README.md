The Quester: Identity Island Digital Demo
Welcome to the digital extension of The Quester Community. This interactive escape room experience is designed to move children from passive viewers to active participants in the "Great Search for Truth."

Project Vision
This demo explores Identity Island, specifically the Cave of Confusion. It serves as a proof of concept for "Discovery Driven Discipleship," where kids solve puzzles and decode messages to internalize Biblical truths.

Key Features in this Demo
Interactive Decoding: Custom-built cryptogram mechanics that teach attention to detail and critical thinking.

Character Mentorship: Integration of Paraclete, the Helper, who provides guidance and wisdom through the darkness of the cave.

Active Learning: Puzzles like the "Tangle of Thoughts" crossword require players to engage deeply with the story and Scripture.

Cross-Platform Accessibility: Optimized for touchscreens and browsers, ensuring it works on tablets, phones, and computers.

The Big Questions
The framework is designed to eventually house adventures for all five of life's biggest questions:

Identity (Who in the world even am I?)

Origins (Where did we all come from?)

Purpose (Does life really have any meaning?)

Morality (How do I know right from wrong?)

Destiny (Is there a future beyond this life?)
