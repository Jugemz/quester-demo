Privacy Policy

Introduction

We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This privacy policy tells you about the types of information that may be collected from you while you visit questercommunity.com and all subdomains (hereby referred to as Quester Community, Quester, or the site), how that information will be used, who can see it, and your rights to review and request changes to it.

Non-Personally Identifiable Information

When you access the site, certain meta information...such as your navigation through the site, browser used, and time spent, along with other similar information...may be stored on our servers and on third-party servers (such as Google Analytics). This information is never connected to any personal information and is treated confidentially. It is collected only to help us understand the preferences of our users and improve their experiences. We do not sell or share this information with any third parties.

Personally Identifiable Information

The site does not collect any personally identifiable information about our users during gameplay. There is no sign-up process for students, and there are no student user accounts. If you choose to join our mailing list or contact us, we will store your email address solely for those purposes.

Use of Cookies

We do not use any form of persistent cookies on the site for student gameplay.

Links to Other Sites

If you click on links to third-party sites, you will be taken to websites we do not control. This policy does not apply to the privacy practices of those websites. Read the privacy policy of other websites carefully. We are not responsible for these third-party sites.

Changes & Contact

This privacy policy is effective as of 4/04/2026. Changes to this policy will be posted here. We will not be actively contacting you with updates, so please check back frequently for any changes. If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via our website.
