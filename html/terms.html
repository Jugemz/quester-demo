Site Description

Quester Community is a series of educational "escape rooms" designed for specific grade levels. These escape rooms are purchased by and assigned by teachers. In each escape room, small teams of students work through various literacy challenges to unlock all the codes before time runs out. Each escape room contains colorful artwork, quirky characters, and hidden lore.
